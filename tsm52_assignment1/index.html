About

Based in Pittsburgh, PA, the alternative band Getaway Driver punches through the basement rock scene with powerful lyrics and a unique genre-hopping sound. Consisting of Trevor Hough on vocals, Travis Mindel on lead guitar, Will Berick on rhythm guitar and bass, Henry Edmunds on bass and drums, and Ethan Cope on drums and vocals, Getaway Driver's versatility is its strength.
